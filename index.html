Mission

Project Black Raven is a legal and technical advocacy initiative focused on dismantling exploitative guardianship systems, defending digital autonomy, and restoring constitutional rights.

Operations

Our work includes live demonstrations of security vulnerabilities, legal document compilation, and strategic policy analysis. We collaborate with families, attorneys, and researchers to expose systemic failures and build resilient counter-narratives.

Contact

To request support, submit documentation, or initiate collaboration, please reach out through secure channels. We prioritize verified cases, credible evidence, and mission-aligned partnerships.
